Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

1. Data We Process

PayVerifyPK processes uploaded screenshots and pasted SMS text only to run fraud detection and verification checks.

2. Screenshot Handling

Uploaded screenshots are temporarily processed for OCR and signal checks, then deleted from temporary storage after analysis.

3. SMS Text Handling

Pasted SMS text is parsed to extract amount, sender name, and transaction identifiers for verification purposes.

4. Retention

We avoid long-term storage of user-submitted financial proof content. Usage counters and lightweight logs may be retained for service stability and abuse prevention.

5. Third-Party Links

Support options may open third-party services such as WhatsApp or email clients. Their privacy policies apply separately.
